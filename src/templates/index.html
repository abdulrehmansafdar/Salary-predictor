About This Tool

This salary predictor uses a Linear Regression model trained on real-world data to estimate your potential salary based on years of professional experience.

After generating your prediction, our AI assistant powered by Google's Gemini API will provide personalized career insights tailored to your experience level.

Model Information

  • Algorithm: Linear Regression
  • MSE: 49,830,096.86
  • Dataset: Salary dataset with Years of Experience
  • AI Integration: Google Gemini 2.0

How It Works

  1. Enter your years of professional experience
  2. Our model calculates an estimated salary based on market data
  3. The AI provides personalized insights about your career stage
  4. Share your results or make another prediction

Note: This tool provides estimates based on available data and should be used as a reference point only.
